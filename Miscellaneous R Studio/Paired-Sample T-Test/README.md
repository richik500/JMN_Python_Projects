# Productivity Before vs After Training | Paired T-Test in R
This repository contains a Paired Sample T-Test project using R to analyze whether employee productivity changed significantly after training.

## Objective
To compare productivity levels:
- Before Training
- After Training
and determine whether training improved performance.

## Dataset Used
paired_t_test.xlsx
Contains paired observations of employee productivity before and after training.

## Tools Used
- R
- RStudio
- Excel

## Analysis Performed
- Difference Calculation
- Histogram of Differences
- Density Curve
- Paired Sample T-Test

## Statistical Method
Paired T-Test is used when the same subjects are measured twice.

## Hypothesis
H0: Mean productivity before training = Mean productivity after training
H1: Mean productivity before training ≠ Mean productivity after training

## Significance Level
α = 0.05
